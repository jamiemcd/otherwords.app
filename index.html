The word guessing game you play with four or more people

You are the wordsmith. Get your teammates to say the phrase — but remember, you can only use other words.

It's super fun! And unlike pass–and–play games, each player has the Other Words app running on their iPhone, iPad, or iPod Touch.

If you've enjoyed classic board games such as Taboo, Catch Phrase, or Guesstures, then you already know the basics of how to play. Other Words has some important differences though.

  • Every player runs the app on their own device.
  • The wordsmith can share any word guessed correctly and it will be shown on everyone's screen. This allows for phrases longer than one or two words — similar to the gameshow Wheel of Fortune.
  • Common words such as "the", "it", and "is" are automatically shared.
  • The opposing team can challenge and everyone casts their vote to determine if a rule was broken.
  • The timer can be set for 60, 75, or 90 seconds.
  • You can choose 1, 2, or 3 rounds.
  • Have kids versus parents by enabling the separate phrase packs per team feature.
