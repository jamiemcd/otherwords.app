The word guessing game you play with four or more people

You are the wordsmith. Get your teammates to say the phrase — but remember, you can only use other words.

It's super fun! And unlike all those pass–and–play games, each player has the Other Words app running on their iPhone, iPad, or iPod Touch. This enables everyone to see the correct words as they are guessed. It also lets the opposing team challenge if a rule is broken.
